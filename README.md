## ğŸ¨ Customization

### Personal Information
Update the following files with your information:

- **Hero Section** (`src/components/Hero.tsx`): Change your name, title, and description
- **About Section** (`src/components/About.tsx`): Update your story, stats, and personal details
- **Skills Section** (`src/components/Skills.tsx`): Modify your skills and proficiency levels
- **Projects Section** (`src/components/Projects.tsx`): Add your own projects with descriptions
- **Experience Section** (`src/components/Experience.tsx`): Update your work history and education
- **Contact Section** (`src/components/Contact.tsx`): Add your contact information and social links

### Styling
- **Colors**: Modify the color scheme in `tailwind.config.js`
- **Fonts**: Change fonts in `src/index.css`
- **Animations**: Adjust animation timings in component files

### Content
- **Images**: Replace placeholder images with your own
- **Links**: Update all social media and project links
- **Resume**: Add your actual resume file and update the download link
